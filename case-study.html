Challenge

Enterprise customers often had multiple cloud, security, infrastructure, and reporting initiatives moving at the same time. Each account required clear ownership, consistent communication, executive-ready reporting, risk tracking, budget awareness, technical coordination, and a roadmap that connected technology work to business outcomes.

Approach

Alex acted as the connective layer between customers, account executives, Microsoft resources, architects, consultants, support teams, and delivery leaders. He managed recurring touchpoints, translated technical priorities into business-facing updates, and kept stakeholders aligned on open actions, risks, milestones, and next-step decisions.

01

Managed a complex multi-account portfolio

Led roadmap discussions, governance reviews, executive updates, and cross-functional delivery alignment while maintaining account visibility through status reports, action trackers, risk summaries, utilization updates, and customer follow-up documentation.

02

Delivered executive QBRs and value reviews

Connected service activity, operational performance, risk trends, utilization, security posture, and roadmap recommendations to customer priorities so leaders could see delivered value and future investment needs.

03

Protected budget and delivery discipline

Tracked utilization, project health, scope alignment, delivery risks, and resource needs to reduce surprises and support better decisions around priorities, surge services, and delivery commitments.

04

Drove expansion through customer need discovery

Identified expansion opportunities by listening for workflow gaps, technical blockers, security concerns, modernization needs, and capacity constraints during recurring account conversations.
